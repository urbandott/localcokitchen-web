FAQ

Common questions

Answers based on the current LocalCoKitchen MVP product direction.

Where is LocalCoKitchen launching?

LocalCoKitchen is Texas-first, starting with Dallas-area marketplace operations and room to expand into other Texas cities.

Who can sell food?

Independent home cooks can apply. LocalCoKitchen admins review identity, profile details, and applicable permits or certifications before activation.

How are allergens handled?

Cooks provide ingredient lists, accurate food descriptions, and allergen disclosures such as milk, eggs, fish, shellfish, tree nuts, peanuts, wheat, and soy.

Can customers pay cash?

No. The PRD states all MVP payments are processed inside LocalCoKitchen.

When is the pickup address shown?

The PRD model shows zip code first. The exact address is visible from two hours before pickup through two hours after pickup completion.

LocalCoKitchen connects customers with trusted independent cooks for homemade meals and scheduled local pickups.
