FAQ

Common questions

How LocalCoKitchen works for customers, cooks, pickup, food details, and launch access.

Customers

Ordering homemade food

How does LocalCoKitchen work?

Enter your ZIP code, browse nearby menu drops, review dish details, order ahead, and pick up from the cook during the listed pickup window.

Is LocalCoKitchen delivery or pickup?

LocalCoKitchen is currently built around scheduled local pickup. Menu pages show pickup availability so customers can plan before ordering.

Where is LocalCoKitchen launching?

LocalCoKitchen is Texas-first, starting with Dallas-area marketplace operations and room to expand into other Texas cities.

Can I search by cuisine, dish, or dietary needs?

The marketplace is designed for discovery by dish, cuisine, distance, rating, dietary preferences, and allergen information as more approved cooks add menus.

Food and pickup

Before you order

How are allergens handled?

Cooks provide ingredient lists, accurate descriptions, and allergen disclosures such as milk, eggs, fish, shellfish, tree nuts, peanuts, wheat, and soy.

When is the exact pickup address shown?

Customers see the cook's ZIP code first. Exact pickup details are shown closer to the pickup window so cook privacy is protected until the order is near fulfillment.

Are meals ready to eat?

Each cook controls their dish details. Review the listing for serving notes, storage guidance, pickup timing, and whether the item is intended to be eaten immediately or reheated.

Can customers pay cash?

No. LocalCoKitchen is designed for in-platform ordering and payments so orders, pickup details, and customer records stay tied to the marketplace flow.

Cooks

Selling on LocalCoKitchen

Who can sell food?

Independent cooks can apply. LocalCoKitchen reviews submitted profile details, training proof, and any applicable permit or certification documents before activation.

What do I need to apply as a cook?

You need a customer account, legal name, US phone number, pickup address, ZIP code, proof of basic food handler training, and optional permit or certification files if they apply.

Can I build my shop before approval?

After submitting a cook application, My shop appears in your account menu so you can prepare storefront details, pickup windows, and menu items while your documents are reviewed.

Who controls menu pricing and availability?

Cooks control their own menu items, pricing, inventory, pickup windows, preorder timing, and fulfillment operations.

LocalCoKitchen connects customers with trusted independent cooks for homemade meals and scheduled local pickups.
