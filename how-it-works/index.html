How it works

From menu discovery to scheduled pickup.

Customers browse, add items to cart, sign in, pay online, receive confirmation, and get pickup instructions close to the pickup time.

1

Browse local cooks

Search by dish, cuisine, cook, dietary tags, distance, and rating.

2

Order securely

Payments happen inside LocalCoKitchen. Cash payments are not part of the MVP model.

3

Coordinate pickup

Messaging opens after order placement and pickup reminders are sent through email or SMS.

4

Review verified orders

Only verified buyers can leave reviews tied to completed orders.
