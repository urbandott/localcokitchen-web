Mission

Help home cooks become trusted local food businesses.

LocalCoKitchen exists to make authentic homemade food easier to discover while giving independent cooks a path to monetize their skill, grow recurring demand, and operate with clearer marketplace standards.

Long-term vision

Build a licensed micro-chef ecosystem that helps talented home cooks transition toward professional food businesses and commercial kitchen operations.
