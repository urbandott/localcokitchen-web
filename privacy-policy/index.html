Privacy Policy

Placeholder privacy policy for the LocalCoKitchen MVP. Replace this page with attorney-reviewed privacy language before production launch.

Information we collect

LocalCoKitchen may collect account details such as name, email address, selected role, marketing preferences, authentication information, cook onboarding information, order information, messages, reviews, and support requests.

How we use information

We use information to create accounts, support authentication, operate marketplace discovery, process orders, coordinate pickups, send transactional notifications, review cook applications, handle disputes, improve safety, and communicate launch updates or promotions when permitted.

Marketing preferences

If you opt in to deals and promotions, LocalCoKitchen may use your email address and account preferences to send promotional messages. You may opt out of promotional messages later, while still receiving necessary account or order communications.

Sharing

We may share limited information with service providers that help operate authentication, hosting, analytics, payments, notifications, fraud prevention, customer support, and marketplace operations. Pickup details may be shared between customers and cooks as needed to fulfill orders.

Cook and food information

Public cook profiles may include cook names, biography, cuisine type, ratings, review counts, menu previews, and pickup zip code. Private details such as exact pickup address should only be shown according to marketplace rules.

Security and retention

We use reasonable safeguards to protect personal information and retain information as needed to operate the marketplace, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce policies.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to info@localcokitchen.com.
