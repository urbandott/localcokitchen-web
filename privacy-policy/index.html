Privacy Policy

This policy explains how LocalCoKitchen collects, uses, shares, and protects information for customers, cooks, applicants, waitlist members, and visitors.

Last updated: May 30, 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to LocalCoKitchen websites, account features, cook application flows, marketplace tools, waitlist forms, menu pages, profile pages, communications, and related services that link to this policy.

By using LocalCoKitchen, you agree that we may collect and use information as described here and in our Terms of Service.

2. Information you provide

We collect information you choose to provide, which may include:

  • Account information such as name, email address, phone number, password authentication data, profile photo, and account preferences.
  • Waitlist information such as email address and whether you are interested as a customer or home cook.
  • Cook application information such as legal name, phone number, pickup address, pickup ZIP code, food handler training proof, permits, certifications, profile details, shop details, and menu information.
  • Marketplace information such as orders, menu items, pricing, inventory, pickup windows, reviews, ratings, messages, support requests, and dispute information.
  • Uploaded files and images such as profile pictures, cook documents, cook profile images, menu item images, and related metadata.

3. Information collected automatically

When you use the site, we and our service providers may collect device and usage information such as IP address, browser type, pages viewed, referring pages, approximate location inferred from IP address, timestamps, clicks, session information, and error logs.

We use cookies, local storage, analytics tags, and similar technologies to support authentication, remember preferences, understand site usage, improve performance, protect the service, and measure marketing or launch interest.

4. Information from service providers

We may receive information from vendors that help us operate LocalCoKitchen, including authentication providers, database and storage providers, analytics tools, email providers, hosting providers, payment processors, fraud prevention services, and customer support tools.

If marketplace payments are enabled, payment information may be processed by a third-party payment processor. LocalCoKitchen should not store full payment card numbers unless expressly stated in a future version of this policy.

5. How we use information

  • Create and manage accounts, authentication, profiles, and account menus.
  • Operate marketplace discovery, menus, shop pages, orders, payments, pickup coordination, reviews, and support.
  • Review cook applications, documents, training proof, permits, certifications, shop details, and menu submissions.
  • Send transactional messages about accounts, applications, orders, pickup, security, support, and policy changes.
  • Send launch updates or promotional messages when permitted by your preferences and applicable law.
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, spam, security incidents, policy violations, and unsafe marketplace activity.
  • Improve, debug, measure, and develop LocalCoKitchen products, pages, features, and communications.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms, policies, and rights.

6. How information is shared

We may share information as needed to operate the marketplace and provide the service:

  • With cooks and customers to coordinate orders, pickup, messages, reviews, and support.
  • With service providers that process information for hosting, authentication, database, storage, email, analytics, payment, fraud prevention, support, and operations.
  • With payment processors and financial partners to process payments, payouts, refunds, chargebacks, taxes, and fraud checks.
  • With legal, safety, regulatory, or law enforcement parties when we believe disclosure is required or appropriate.
  • With a successor or potential successor in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, bankruptcy, or sale of assets.
  • With your consent or at your direction.

7. Public marketplace information

Some information may be visible to other users or the public, such as cook display names, shop names, profile photos, biographies, cuisine types, menu descriptions, menu images, pickup ZIP codes, ratings, review counts, and public reviews.

Exact pickup addresses, phone numbers, private cook documents, and application files are intended to be limited according to marketplace rules and operational need.

8. Cook documents and uploads

Cook applicants may upload food handler training proof, permits, certifications, profile photos, and menu images. We use these materials to review applications, operate shop features, verify marketplace requirements, display approved content, provide support, and maintain safety and compliance records.

Do not upload documents or images that contain information unrelated to your cook application or marketplace use.

9. Location and pickup information

We collect pickup addresses and ZIP codes from cooks and may collect ZIP codes, addresses, or location-related information from customers when needed for marketplace discovery, ordering, pickup, support, fraud prevention, and safety.

Customers may see a cook's ZIP code before ordering. Exact pickup details may be shown closer to the pickup window or otherwise as needed to fulfill an order.

10. Communications and marketing

We may send transactional communications about your account, cook application, orders, pickup windows, support requests, security, and policy updates. These messages are part of the service and may not always include an unsubscribe option.

If you join the launch list or opt in to marketing, we may send launch updates, promotions, surveys, or product news. You can opt out of promotional emails by using unsubscribe instructions when available or contacting us.

11. Cookies, analytics, and local storage

LocalCoKitchen uses analytics and browser technologies, including Google Analytics and local browser storage, to understand usage, remember form or preview state, support authentication, and improve the site.

Your browser may let you block or delete cookies and local storage. Some features may not work properly if these technologies are disabled.

12. Data retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service, operate accounts, review cook applications, fulfill orders, resolve disputes, meet legal and tax obligations, enforce policies, prevent fraud, maintain security, and improve LocalCoKitchen.

Retention periods may vary based on the type of information, account status, marketplace activity, legal obligations, and operational needs.

13. Security

We use reasonable technical, administrative, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. No online service, database, storage system, or transmission method is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

14. Your choices

You may update certain account or profile information through available account features. You may request access, correction, deletion, or other privacy assistance by contacting us. We may need to verify your identity and may retain information where required or permitted by law.

You can opt out of promotional communications, adjust browser cookie settings, and choose what optional information you provide. Required information may be necessary to use certain features, place orders, or apply as a cook.

15. Children

LocalCoKitchen is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can take appropriate steps.

16. State privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability, or limits on certain processing of personal information. We will respond to applicable requests as required by law.

LocalCoKitchen does not intend to sell personal information for money. If future features involve targeted advertising or data sharing regulated as a "sale" or "sharing" under applicable privacy laws, we will update this policy and provide required choices.

17. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as LocalCoKitchen evolves. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Continued use of the service after an update means the updated policy applies.

18. Contact

Questions or privacy requests can be sent through the Contact us page or by email at info@localcokitchen.com.
