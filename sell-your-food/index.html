Sell your Food

Publish menus after approval and build repeat local demand.

The PRD defines cooks as independent sellers who control menus, pricing, inventory, pickup availability, and fulfillment. LocalCoKitchen provides discovery, ordering, and payment tools.

Cook onboarding

Cook profiles are designed for identity verification, profile photos, biographies, pickup zip codes, and permit or certification uploads where applicable.

Menu control

Create categories, upload item photos, set prices, define quantities, choose allergens and dietary tags, and mark items as sold out.

Simple marketplace pricing

The MVP model in the PRD is a $20 monthly cook subscription plus a 5% marketplace commission per order.
