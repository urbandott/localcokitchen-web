Sell your Food

Apply to become a LocalCoKitchen cook.

Start as a customer account, then apply here. Cooks must complete a basic food handler training course and upload proof before creating a shop and menu.

Training proof required

Upload proof that you completed a basic food handler training course. PDF, PNG, JPG, or WebP files up to 5 MB are accepted.

Independent seller model

You control your menu, pricing, inventory, pickup availability, and fulfillment operations.

Shop tools after signup

After submission, My shop appears in your account menu so you can configure storefront details, pickup windows, and menu items.
