Before you apply

What you need for cook signup.

LocalCoKitchen is designed around independent sellers. You are responsible for your food operation, local requirements, availability, and fulfillment.

  • Completed basic food handler training proof.
  • Legal name, US phone number, pickup address, and 5-digit ZIP code.
  • Optional permit or certification upload if it applies to your food business.
  • PDF, PNG, JPG, or WebP documents up to 5 MB.
