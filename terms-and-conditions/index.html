Cooks are responsible for accurate menu descriptions, ingredient lists, allergen disclosures, pickup availability, food preparation, labeling, permits, certifications, taxes, and compliance with local, state, and federal requirements.

Orders are expected to be paid through LocalCoKitchen. Cooks define preorder cutoffs, quantities, and pickup windows. Customers are responsible for arriving within the scheduled pickup window and reviewing listing details before ordering.

Some foods may be prepared in home kitchens or facilities that are not subject to routine state inspection. Ingredient and allergen information is provided by independent cooks, and cross-contact risks may exist.
